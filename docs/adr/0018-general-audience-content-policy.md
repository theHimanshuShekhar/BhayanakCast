# ADR 0018: Operate as a general-audience community

- **Status:** Accepted
- **Date:** 2026-07-10

## Context

Any Discord-authenticated account may participate in public screen-sharing rooms. The platform needs an explicit content boundary consistent with its report and sanction workflows.

## Decision

BhayanakCast is a general-audience community. Sexual or explicit content and graphic or illegal content are prohibited. V1 has no separate age gate beyond Discord authentication; reports, Host controls, and Platform Admin sanctions enforce the content boundary.

## Consequences

- The room-creation flow does not offer an adult/mature-room classification.
- Reporting and moderation copy must clearly distinguish prohibited content from merely unwanted content.
- A future mature-content experience requires a separate age/admission/moderation decision.
