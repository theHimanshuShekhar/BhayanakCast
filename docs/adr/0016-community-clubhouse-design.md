# ADR 0016: Design the UI as a community clubhouse

- **Status:** Accepted
- **Date:** 2026-07-10

## Context

The rewrite replaces the inherited operational “Live Signal Deck” visual system. The new visual direction needs a concrete usage scene before choosing color, type, density, or motion.

## Decision

The product UI is guided by a community-clubhouse scene: people browse an active public community and move between rooms. The interface should feel welcoming, lively, and easy to scan rather than like a broadcast studio or a dense operations console.

## Consequences

- Discovery, room presence, and social movement between rooms should be legible at a glance.
- The room mosaic remains important, but the surrounding UI should support belonging and conversation rather than compete with media.
- The detailed palette, typography, information density, and motion language remain design-brief decisions.
